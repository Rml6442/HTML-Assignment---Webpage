Why was this website started?

It started because of the owner's care for penguins and detailing everything about them. There can never be too many facts about your favorite animal!

How exotic can the multiple species of penguins be? Don't they all live in Antartica?

On the contrary, they live in a plethora of places other than that ice desert. Other places such as Australia, Argentina, and South Africa, just to name a few.
